Bonfire

The project started in 2024 with the idea of bringing human connection to the most fundamental place where it originated, a bonfire. The place where people with similar interests gather to share and discuss.

Once we apply technology and the advancement of AI and AGI, bonfire wants to create the right environment to increase the potential of human interaction. This allows humans to enhance our species' capabilities in the new technological era

To achieve this, Bonfire wants to maximize the human experience based on topics of interest and to increase the interest of others. Additionally, it aims to use AI to provide us with new tools and resources.
